Our Story

Although we had met briefly before, we 'officially' met while attending the 21st birthday of bridesmaid Kaylen Smeets, married to groomsman Joey Smeets, in 2011. After graduating college, we moved to Louisville, Kentucky. After a short year and a half, Tommy's job required us to move to Western Massachusetts where we surprisingly found the best falafel we ever had at a restaurant right outside the UMass campus. After a couple year stay in New England, we decided that it was time to return home to the Midwest. Emily got a job at Aldi's corporate office in Batavia, Illinois and Tommy enrolled in graduate school at NIU working towards a master's degree in computer science. During this time, we moved in with Tommy's parents in Aurora, Illinois. After Tommy graduated in May 2017, they moved into their own apartment in Aurora and Tommy began working as a Software Engineer for Ace Hardware in Oak Brook, Illinois. Outside of work, the we love to spend time with our dog Bourbon, cook together, and we are looking forward to traveling more. We got engaged on November 18th, 2018 at the Crystal Gardens at Navy Pier in Chicago, Illinois. Photos of the engagement were taken by Emily's sister, Grace Klosterman. We promptly celebrated the occasion by visiting Revolution Brewery and Pequod's Pizza.
