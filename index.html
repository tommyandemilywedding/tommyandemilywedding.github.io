Our Story

Although they had met briefly before, Emily and Tommy officially met while attending the 21st birthday of bridesmaid Kaylen Smeets, married to groomsman Joey Smeets, in 2011. After graduating college, Emily and Tommy settled in Louisville, Kentucky. After a short year and a half, Tommy's job required them to move out to Western Massachusetts where they surprisingly found they best falafel they ever had at a restaurant right outside the UMass campus. After a couple year stay in New England, the couple decided that it was time to return home to the Midwest. Emily got a job at Aldi's corporate office in Batavia, Illinois and Tommy enrolled in graduate school at NIU working towards a master's degree in computer science. During this time, the couple moved in with Tommy's parents in Aurora, Illinois. After Tommy graduated in May 2017, they moved into their own apartment in Aurora and Tommy began working as a Software Engineer for Ace Hardware in Oak Brook, Illinois. Outside of work, the couple loves to spend time with their dog Bourbon, cooking together, and is looking forward to traveling more. They were engaged on November 18th, 2018 at the Crystal Gardens at Navy Pier in Chicago, Illinois. Photos of the engagement were taken by Emily's sister, Grace Klosterman. They promptly celebrated the occasion by visiting Revolution Brewery and Pequod's Pizza.
